Our Story

From a passionate dream to a premium café experience

Founded by Arun Patil

Wild Crest began with Arun's passion for authentic South Indian coffee and his dream to create a space where people could experience premium coffee in a warm, welcoming environment.

As a coffee lover and entrepreneur, Arun traveled across Karnataka, discovering the rich coffee plantations of Chikmagalur. He was captivated by the quality and flavor of the beans, and committed to bringing this authentic taste to urban coffee lovers.

"Coffee is more than a drink—it's an experience that brings people together, fuels conversations, and creates memories." — Arun Patil
Founder & CEO, Wild Crest
Arun Patil at Chikmagalur coffee plantation

Our Philosophy

What makes Wild Crest special

Directly Sourced

Our coffee beans come directly from Chikmagalur, Karnataka, known for its rich coffee plantations. We work directly with local farmers to ensure quality and fair trade practices.

Supporting Local

We're committed to supporting local farmers and promoting authentic South Indian coffee culture. Every cup you enjoy helps sustain traditional coffee farming communities.

Community Focused

Wild Crest is designed as a welcoming space for everyone—travelers, students, professionals, and night riders seeking quality coffee and a relaxing atmosphere.

The Wild Crest Experience

We've created more than just a café—it's a destination where premium coffee meets gourmet food in a space designed for comfort and connection.

  • Warm, inviting atmosphere with comfortable seating
  • Knowledgeable baristas crafting perfect coffee
  • Freshly prepared meals using quality ingredients
  • Welcoming environment for both quick visits and long stays
  • Special highway café open 24/7 for travelers

Our Vision

To create premium café experiences that celebrate authentic South Indian coffee culture while providing warm, welcoming spaces for community connection. We envision Wild Crest as more than a coffee shop—it's a destination where travelers find respite, students find inspiration, professionals find focus, and everyone finds exceptional coffee.
